# Week 1 – Research Fundamentals

This folder contains my Week 1 work for the CariSurg MedTech programme.

## 📌 Contents

- **Problem Statement**  
  Defines the clinical challenge of variability in emergency department triage decision-making in a Caribbean setting.

- **Paper Summaries**  
  Critical summaries of five research papers on AI-assisted triage, including analysis of methods, results, and limitations.

## 🧠 Key Focus

- AI in emergency department triage  
- Machine learning for clinical decision support  
- Identification of research gaps  
- Application of AI in resource-constrained healthcare environments
